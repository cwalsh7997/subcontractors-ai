Common Challenges

Roofing Contractors Face These Every Day

📐 Roof Area & Detail Complexity

Commercial roofs aren't flat rectangles. Penetrations, curbs, pitch changes, parapets, drains, and expansion joints all require detailed measurement and unique flashing. Miss a skylight curb or undercount penetrations and you're absorbing the extra material and labor on a lump-sum bid.

💰 Material Price & Availability Swings

TPO, EPDM, modified bitumen, and metal panel pricing fluctuates with petroleum and steel markets. Insulation board prices follow different curves. Bid in January with January pricing and you're exposed by April install. One material substitution without approval can void manufacturer warranties.

🌧️ Weather & Schedule Risk

Roofing is the most weather-dependent trade. Contracts that don't account for weather delays shift schedule liquidated damages to you. A two-week rain delay on a fast-track job can trigger $1K/day LD clauses that weren't priced into your bid.

⚠️ Warranty & Liability Exposure

Manufacturer warranties (20-year, 30-year NDL) have strict installation requirements. Contract language often makes the roofing sub the sole guarantor of warranty performance. One improperly sealed penetration discovered 3 years later can trigger a full warranty claim against you.
