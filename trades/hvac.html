Common Challenges

HVAC Contractors Struggle with Profitability

🔧 Complex Load Calculations Take Hours

HVAC design is technical — you need to calculate heating/cooling loads based on building area, insulation, exposure, local climate, and occupancy. Manual calculations are time-consuming and error-prone. Wrong load estimates lead to undersized/oversized systems and margin loss.

💰 Equipment Pricing Changes Weekly

Compressors, heat exchangers, and electrical components swing in price constantly. Bid a job in January at Q1 prices, then watch margins evaporate by June. Without real-time pricing from manufacturers and suppliers, you're flying blind.

⏱️ Inconsistent Labor Estimation

Installation hours vary dramatically by job type — simple replacement is different from new construction with custom ductwork. Your crew's productivity changes based on space complexity and system type. Gut-feel estimating leaves $5K-20K on the table.

⚠️ Warranty Liability & Compliance Traps

HVAC contracts often include traps around system performance guarantees, efficiency warranties, and liability for energy claims. Miss a clause and you're on the hook for a year of energy cost claims — potentially $10K+.
