Common Challenges

Mechanical / HVAC Contractors Face These Every Day

🌡️ Ductwork Takeoff Complexity

Commercial HVAC means hundreds of duct runs across multiple zones — each with different sizes, insulation requirements, and fitting counts. Missing a transition, underestimating hangers, or miscounting flex runs means you're absorbing the cost on a fixed-price deal.

💰 Equipment Lead Times & Price Volatility

RTUs, chillers, and VRF systems have lead times stretching 16-24 weeks. Prices shift with steel and refrigerant costs. If your bid is based on a quote that expires before award, you're exposed. Real-time equipment pricing and lead time tracking is critical.

🔧 Coordination Conflicts with Other Trades

Mechanical rough-in conflicts with electrical conduit, plumbing stacks, and fire protection. Every coordination clash means rework, schedule delays, and finger-pointing. Without documenting your install sequence, you'll absorb costs that aren't yours.

⚠️ Energy Code & Commissioning Requirements

ASHRAE 90.1, local energy codes, and commissioning requirements add layers of compliance. Contracts often shift full commissioning liability to the mechanical sub. A failed TAB report or missed energy code requirement can create $20K+ in rework.
