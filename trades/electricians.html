Common Challenges

Electrical Contractors Lose Thousands Every Year

⚡ Scope Extraction Takes Days

Manually reviewing electrical plans, specs, and architectural documents to understand panel requirements, conduit runs, and equipment schedules is time-consuming and error-prone. Your team spends hours extracting quantities instead of selling.

💰 Bidding Blind on Material Costs

Electrical supply costs fluctuate weekly. You bid at Q1 pricing, but by the time you order in Q3, margins have evaporated. Without real-time cost data, you're guessing on wire, breakers, transformers, and labor productivity.

⚙️ Labor Estimation Inefficiencies

Every job is different — residential wiring vs. commercial panels vs. industrial systems. Scaling labor hours across trade complexity and crew experience is inconsistent. You underbid high-complexity work or overbid simple jobs.

📋 Contract Risk Exposure

Hidden change-order triggers, unexpected site conditions clauses, and unfavorable payment terms hide in contract language. You don't catch them until disputes arise. One missed clause can wipe out 10 jobs' worth of profit.
