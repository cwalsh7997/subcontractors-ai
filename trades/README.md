## Design Features

All pages include:
- **Professional Navigation**: Fixed nav with logo, links to main site pages (Find Subs, GC Ratings, AI Tools, Pricing), Login/Sign Up buttons
- **Brand Consistency**: Navy (#0f1729), Blue (#2563eb), Amber/Orange (#f59e0b) color scheme matching main site
- **Inter Font**: Google Fonts Inter family (400-900 weights)
- **Hero Section**: Gradient background, trade-specific headline with orange accent, subheader, dual CTA buttons
- **Pain Points Section**: 4 trade-specific pain points with left border accent (2-column grid)
- **Solution Section**: 4 feature items with icons, detailed descriptions
- **How It Works**: 3-step process with numbered circles
- **Social Proof**: 3 testimonials from realistic trade practitioners with 5-star ratings
- **CTA Sections**: Multiple call-to-action buttons directing to signup/pricing
- **Footer**: Consistent footer with product links, company links, resources, trade links
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile breakpoints for tablet/mobile viewing
- **JSON-LD Schema**: Organization and WebPage structured data for SEO

## Content Specifications

- **Electricians**: Focus on electrical systems, panel requirements, code compliance, hurricane-resistant wiring
- **Plumbing**: Focus on fixture schedules, water/drain lines, warranty liability, water damage exposure
- **HVAC**: Focus on load calculations, equipment sizing, efficiency claims, performance warranties
- **Concrete**: Focus on volume calculations, reinforcement, subgrade liability, weather delays
- **Steel**: Focus on tonnage extraction, mill pricing, fabrication complexity, shop drawing resubmittals
- **Glass/Glazing**: Focus on square footage, curtain wall systems, hurricane impact, performance specs

Each page targets realistic pain points and solutions specific to that trade's business model.
