Common Challenges

Glass & Glazing Contractors Face These Every Day

📐 Complex Square Footage Calculations

Curtain wall, storefront glazing, and interior glass systems require calculating rough openings, mullion layouts, and panel sizes from architectural drawings. Manual takeoff is tedious and error-prone. Missing one floor or miscounting panels directly hits profit on fixed-price bids.

💰 Glass & Hardware Pricing Volatility

Glass pricing fluctuates with raw material costs. Hardware and aluminum frames follow different pricing curves. Bid the job in Q1 at Q1 prices and you're locked in while Q3 costs have shifted 15-20%. Without real-time supplier pricing, you're guessing.

⏱️ Labor Varies by System Type

Curtain wall installation labor is fundamentally different from storefront, which differs from interior partitions. High-rise work with staging costs differently than ground floor. Without tracking actual labor by system type, you're estimating blind.

⚠️ Hurricane Impact & Compliance Traps

Many projects require hurricane impact glass, soundproofing specs, or fire-rated glazing. Contract language can hide liability for failing to meet performance specs. One missed requirement can create $15K+ in rework liability.
