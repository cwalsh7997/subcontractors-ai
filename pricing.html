Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers.

Can I start on Free and upgrade to Pro later? ▼
Yes, absolutely! Start with our free plan and upgrade to Pro anytime. Your profile, projects, and data carry over seamlessly. There's no penalty for upgrading — many subs start free to try the platform, then upgrade when they see the ROI from the AI tools.
What's included in the AI Bid Assistant? ▼
Upload your project plans, specs, and drawings (PDF, DWG, images). Our AI extracts the scope relevant to your trade, identifies quantities, flags key specifications, and provides estimated cost ranges. You get a structured breakdown that's ready to bid from. No more manual scope extraction or guessing.
Is there a long-term contract? ▼
No contracts, ever. Cancel your subscription anytime with just a few clicks. We work on month-to-month billing, and we only make money if we provide you with value. Your success is our success.
What happens during the 14-day free trial? ▼
You get full access to every feature in your chosen plan for 14 days. No charge until the trial ends. Cancel anytime during the trial and you won't be billed. We'll send you a reminder before the trial expires so there are no surprises.
How does the compliance dashboard work? ▼
Upload your insurance certificates, licenses, and certifications. The dashboard tracks renewal dates and automatically alerts you 30 days before anything expires. You'll never lose a bid because docs went out of date. Available on Pro and above.
Can I manage my team on Subcontractors.ai? ▼
Yes! Business plan includes team management for up to 5 seats. Each team member gets their own login, and you control permissions (who can bid, who can rate GCs, who can see analytics, etc.). Enterprise plan gives you unlimited seats.
How does GC Ratings work? Are reviews anonymous? ▼
GC Ratings lets you rate and review general contractors you've worked with. Think Glassdoor for contractors. You can rate payment speed, change order fairness, communication, safety, and more. Anonymous reviews are welcome — your company name is never published. We verify reviewers are real subs but keep identities private.
