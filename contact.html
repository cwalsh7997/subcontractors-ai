Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Subcontractors.ai

Is Subcontractors.ai free?

+
Yes! Subcontractors.ai offers a free plan forever. You can create a profile, list your company, and access the project board at no cost. We also offer premium features for businesses ready to scale and get more leads.

How do I get listed on the platform?

+
Getting listed is simple. Create a free profile with your company name, trade, location, and contact information. Once verified, you'll appear in search results for general contractors looking for subs in your area. You can add photos, past projects, and licenses to stand out.

Can general contractors use the platform?

+
Absolutely. Subcontractors.ai is built for subs first, but GCs will love it too. We're rolling out dedicated GC features soon, including the ability to post projects, rate subcontractors, and manage your entire bid process in one place. Early access available now.

How do ratings work on your platform?

+
Ratings on Subcontractors.ai come from verified subcontractors who have actually worked together. Anonymous ratings protect your privacy while keeping the system honest. We don't show reviews from unverified accounts, so you know every rating is legitimate and came from a real GC or sub who has used your services.

Do you cover my state?

+
We currently operate in 10 states across the US, with Florida, Texas, California, Arizona, Georgia, North Carolina, Pennsylvania, New York, Colorado, and Michigan as our core markets. We're expanding to new states every month. Check your state availability when you sign up — if we're not there yet, join our waitlist and we'll notify you when we launch.
