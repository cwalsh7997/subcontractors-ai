This directory contains 8 professional PDF templates for construction billing
and payment processing, formatted to match actual AIA (American Institute of 
Architects) document standards used in the construction industry.

INCLUDED TEMPLATES:
=============================================================================

1. AIA G702 - Application and Certificate for Payment
   File: aia-g702-payment-application.pdf
   Purpose: Standard payment application form used to request payment from
   owners based on completed work. Includes contract summary, payment due
   calculations, and certification blocks for contractor and architect.

2. AIA G703 - Continuation Sheet
   File: continuation-sheet-g703.pdf
   Purpose: Line-item detail sheet that accompanies G702. Lists individual
   work items, scheduled values, completion percentages, and running totals.
   Typically used with G702 to detail progress on each contract line item.

3. Conditional Waiver and Release on Progress Payment
   File: conditional-progress-lien-waiver.pdf
   Purpose: Legal form where subcontractors waive lien rights upon receipt
   of progress payment. Conditional = rights restored if payment doesn't clear.

4. Unconditional Waiver and Release on Progress Payment
   File: unconditional-progress-lien-waiver.pdf
   Purpose: Legal form where subcontractors permanently waive lien rights
   immediately upon signing. Unconditional = rights waived regardless of
   payment status. WARNING: Use carefully, only after payment received.

5. Conditional Waiver and Release on Final Payment
   File: conditional-final-lien-waiver.pdf
   Purpose: Final release of all lien rights for final payment. Conditional
   upon receipt and clearing of final payment check.

6. Unconditional Waiver and Release on Final Payment
   File: unconditional-final-lien-waiver.pdf
   Purpose: Permanent final release of all lien rights. Unconditional waiver
   signed immediately upon completion. Complete release of all claims.

7. Change Order Request
   File: change-order-request.pdf
   Purpose: Document used to request modifications to the original contract.
   Includes description of changes, cost/time adjustments, and signature
   blocks for owner, contractor, and architect approval.

8. Monthly Billing Summary
   File: monthly-billing-summary.pdf
   Purpose: Summary report of monthly billing. Includes project info, billing
   summary table (total contract, previous billing, current billing, retainage,
   balance), line item detail, and contractor certification.
