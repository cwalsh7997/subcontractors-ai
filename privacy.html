Privacy Policy

Your data privacy is important to us. Learn how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information about you in several ways to provide and improve our platform:

Account Information

When you create a Subcontractors.ai account, we collect your company name, contact email, phone number, physical business address, trade or specialty, years in business, licensing information, insurance details, and any payment information you provide. We use this to verify your profile, facilitate connections, and enable secure transactions.

Usage Data

We collect information about how you interact with our platform, including which projects you view, which subcontractors you contact, your search history, pages visited, time spent on features, and messages sent through our messaging system. This helps us understand user behavior, improve our platform, and prevent fraud.

Device and Browser Information

We collect technical information including IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and general location (city/region level, not precise). This helps us optimize for different devices, diagnose technical issues, and ensure security.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to remember preferences, maintain sessions, analyze usage patterns, and measure campaign performance. Some cookies are essential for platform functionality, while others are analytical or marketing-related.

Uploaded Content

You may upload photos, documents, testimonials, or other content to your profile or projects. We store this content and any metadata associated with it (file names, upload dates, file sizes) to deliver our services.

Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by secure third-party payment processors. We do not directly store credit card numbers, but we retain transaction records, invoice history, and billing addresses for accounting and fraud prevention purposes.

Communications

When you contact us via email, chat, or our contact form, we collect the content of those messages, your email address, and any attachments. We use this information to respond to your inquiries and improve customer support.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for these specific purposes:

Platform Operation

To create and maintain your account, authenticate users, process payments, send confirmations, and deliver core platform services like project matching, messaging, and ratings.

Subcontractor Matching

To match general contractors and project managers with qualified subcontractors in their area and trade, and to help subcontractors find projects that fit their specialties and capacity.

Communication

To send you service notifications (password resets, account alerts), respond to support requests, notify you of new project opportunities, and send updates about platform improvements or service changes. You can manage email preferences in your account settings.

Safety and Fraud Prevention

To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent activity, unauthorized access, payment fraud, fake reviews, and violations of our Terms of Service.

Analytics and Improvement

To analyze user behavior patterns, identify popular features, understand demand trends, and make data-driven improvements to our platform. All analytics are aggregated and anonymized.

Business Operations

For accounting, auditing, billing, tax compliance, legal requests from law enforcement or courts, and to enforce our Terms of Service.

Marketing and Outreach

To send newsletters, feature updates, industry tips, and promotional information about our paid plans. You can opt out of marketing emails at any time via a link in every email or through your account settings.

3. Information Sharing

We take data sharing seriously and limit it to what's necessary to operate our platform:

Public Profile Information

Your company name, trade/specialty, location (city/state), years in business, ratings, reviews, and any public portfolio items are visible to other users on the platform to facilitate connections. You control what information is public versus private.

Project-Related Sharing

When you apply for or are matched with a project, the project manager and other relevant parties may see your company name, contact information, trade, and relevant qualifications. This is necessary to facilitate the business relationship.

We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. We will never sell your email address, payment information, or account data to marketing companies, data brokers, or any other commercial entity.

Service Providers

We share limited information with trusted third-party service providers who perform functions on our behalf, including: payment processors (to process payments), email service providers (to send communications), analytics platforms (to measure usage), cloud hosting providers (to store data), and customer support tools (to manage inquiries). These providers are contractually obligated to maintain data confidentiality and use the information only for services they provide to us.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request. We will attempt to notify you of such requests unless legally prohibited from doing so.

Business Transfers

If Subcontractors.ai is involved in a merger, acquisition, bankruptcy, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change and any changes to how your data is handled.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL protocols
  • Passwords are hashed and salted; we never store passwords in plain text
  • Sensitive information is stored in encrypted databases with access controls
  • Our servers are hosted on secure, compliant cloud infrastructure
  • We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing
  • Employee access to personal data is restricted to those who need it for their role
  • We maintain incident response procedures for potential data breaches

While we take security seriously, no system is perfectly secure. If we detect a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you as required by law.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to access, control, and delete your information:

Access and Portability

You can request a copy of all personal information we hold about you in a machine-readable format. Email us at hello@subcontractors.ai and we will provide this within 30 days.

Correction

If any of your account information is inaccurate or incomplete, you can update it directly in your account settings or request our assistance.

Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data. We will remove your profile, contact information, and personal details within 30 days. Note that public reviews and ratings you've written may remain (anonymized), and we may retain data as required by law for tax, legal, or compliance purposes. Transaction history is retained for accounting purposes.

Opt-Out

You can opt out of marketing emails by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or adjusting your notification preferences in account settings. You can also disable non-essential cookies in your browser settings, though this may limit some platform functionality.

Do Not Track

Some browsers include a "Do Not Track" feature. We recognize such signals, though we note that universal standards for DNT response have not been established.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies for these purposes:

Essential Cookies

These are necessary for the platform to function: session management, authentication, security, and preventing fraud. You cannot disable these without breaking the platform.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics and similar tools to understand how users interact with our platform, which pages are popular, where users come from, and how long they stay. This helps us improve features and user experience. These cookies are anonymous and don't identify you personally.

Marketing Cookies

We use cookies to track campaign performance, show relevant ads on other websites, and measure conversion rates. You can disable these in your browser settings or through ad preference managers, though you may still see non-targeted ads.

Third-Party Cookies

Third-party service providers (analytics, advertising partners) may also set cookies on our platform. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies for how they use collected data.

7. Children's Privacy

Subcontractors.ai is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 18. If we become aware that we've collected information from a child under 18, we will delete that information promptly. If you believe we've collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately at hello@subcontractors.ai.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the platform after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal information, please contact us:

Subcontractors.ai Privacy Team

Email: hello@subcontractors.ai

Response Time: We typically respond to privacy inquiries within 5 business days.

If you're not satisfied with our response, you may have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
